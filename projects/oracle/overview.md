# Oracle Health & AI

I joined Oracle Health & AI after my undergrad degree in CS. The organization was brand new when I joined - I got somewhat of a startup experience despite being at a very large company. I worked with senior architects on backend design, infrastructure, developing our org's CI/CD practices (which didn't yet exist), and a bit of frontend. I quite enjoyed being an early, core builder. The product continued to rapidly evolve, and I got to touch various different projects, each often quite different than the last.

here i've highlighted a few projects from my time at OHAI. notably, frontend work I did is not shown here, as the product is not public. for some of the services mentioned, i worked on e2e flows, including the frontend, which were typically react/typscript. since I already felt familiar with frontend web development, my time at oracle stretched me to expand further down into the stack, becoming well versed in microservice/API development, working with DAL layers and different types of databases, cloud infratstructure (terraform, primarily), control plane layers, and deployment orchestration. Additionally, the final year of my time at Oracle heavily involved developing standalone agents and agentic architecture.
